                the tragedy of humankind is that <br />
                    our fate to destroy one another is <br />
                    only unavoidable because we seek <br />
                    ourselves annihilation. if we willed <br />
                    <br />
                    to survive together we would have <br />
                    no need for the wanton displeasures <br />
                    of greed. simply gaze at the animal <br />
                    world. from pods of whales to colonies <br />
                    of ants life is a series of obsessions <br />
                    impulsively followed with so many <br />
                    others exactly like us at our side <br />
                    <br />
                    wandering blind in grand choreography. <br />
                    motivated by machinery set in motion <br />
                    before we were even born our industry <br />
                    makes us less than whole. so to find more <br />
                    we turn in circles until stumbling across <br />
                    <br />
                    desires not less than we hoped for. we <br />
                    pledge primal oaths and sanguine spill <br />
                    blood. we declare war to clamor for peace. <br />
                    <br />
                    we shut out the sun’s light to find <br />
                    only ourselves solitary in the dark.
