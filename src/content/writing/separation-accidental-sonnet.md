    <h3><i>for those countless children orphaned by a malevolent State</i></h3>
        <p>
        <sup>1</sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;blue wails surfeit fluorescent<br />
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;mausoleum walls flooded with<br />
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;sheer fear those young<br />
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;tears spend less time<br />
        <sup>5</sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;falling but remain still<br />
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;who do we think<br />
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;we are anyways brutal<br />
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;retribution to follow shortly<br /> 
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;but suffering persists longer<br />
        <sup>10</sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;than we will<br />
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;care because we broke<br />
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;so many<br /> 
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;hearts that can never be<br /> 
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;mended (again)<br />
        </p>
